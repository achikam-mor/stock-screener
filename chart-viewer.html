How to Use

  • Search for any ticker: Enter a stock symbol from the NYSE that we've analyzed.
  • View historical data: See the last year of price history with OHLC candlesticks.
  • Candlestick colors: Green indicates bullish days (close > open), red indicates bearish days (close < open).
  • SMAs: Simple Moving Averages (50, 150, 200) to identify trend direction. Use checkboxes to toggle visibility.
  • Interactive chart: Hover over candles to see exact OHLC values for each day.
