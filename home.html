Welcome to Stock Scanner

This platform provides a complimentary stock screening service for equities traded on the New York Stock Exchange. Our analysis employs a well-established and proven trading methodology based on the Simple Moving Average (SMA) over the past 150 trading days.

Our Screening Criteria

  • Price Distance: We focus on stocks trading within ¬±4% of their SMA150 (both above and below), identifying stocks at key support and resistance levels.
  • Market Capitalization: Only companies with a market cap of $2 billion or more are included, ensuring adequate liquidity and institutional interest.
  • Technical Analysis: We calculate the Average True Range (ATR14) to help you determine optimal position sizing and stop-loss placement.

By examining the price distance from the SMA150 and the Average True Range (ATR), traders can make informed decisions regarding trade entry points and appropriate stop-loss placement. These technical indicators serve as fundamental tools in risk management and position sizing strategies for day trading, swing trading, and long-term investing.
