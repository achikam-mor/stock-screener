Welcome to Stock Screener

This platform provides a complimentary stock screening service for equities traded on the New York Stock Exchange. Our analysis employs a well-established and proven trading methodology based on the Simple Moving Average (SMA) over the past 150 trading days.

By examining the price distance from the SMA150 and the Average True Range (ATR), traders can make informed decisions regarding trade entry points and appropriate stop-loss placement. These technical indicators serve as fundamental tools in risk management and position sizing strategies.
